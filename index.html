Types of Yoga

Explore different styles and find the perfect practice for your journey

Hatha Yoga

A gentle introduction to the most basic yoga postures. Perfect for beginners seeking balance and flexibility.

Vinyasa Yoga

A dynamic practice that links movement with breath. Flow through sequences that build strength and grace.

Ashtanga Yoga

A rigorous style of yoga that follows a specific sequence of postures. Builds incredible strength and stamina.

Kundalini Yoga

A spiritual practice that combines physical postures, breathing techniques, meditation, and chanting.

Yin Yoga

A quiet, meditative practice with poses held for longer periods. Perfect for deep stretching and relaxation.

Restorative Yoga

A deeply relaxing practice using props to support the body in gentle poses for ultimate stress relief.
